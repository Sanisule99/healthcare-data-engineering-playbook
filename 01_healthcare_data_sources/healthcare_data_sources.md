# Healthcare Data Sources

## Electronic Health Records

EHR data include encounters, diagnoses, medications, procedures, notes, orders, and laboratory values.

## Claims Data

Claims data support utilization, cost, quality, and population health analytics.

## FHIR APIs

FHIR APIs allow standardized access to healthcare resources such as Patient, Observation, Condition, and Encounter.

## Public Research Data

Examples include TCGA, GTEx, TCIA, and other public datasets.

## Synthetic Data

Synthetic datasets allow safe demonstration of workflows without exposing PHI.

## Key Questions

- Who owns the data?
- What format is the data in?
- Does it contain PHI?
- What is the intended use?
- What transformations are required?
