# Public Health Measure Dictionary

## BP Screening Rate

**Definition:** Percent of participants screened for blood pressure.

**Numerator:** Number of participants screened for BP  
**Denominator:** Total participants  
**Formula:** screened_bp / total_participants

---

## High BP Identification Rate

**Definition:** Percent of participants screened who were identified with high BP.

**Numerator:** high_bp_identified  
**Denominator:** screened_bp  
**Formula:** high_bp_identified / screened_bp

---

## Referral Completion Rate

**Definition:** Percent of participants with high BP who completed referral.

**Numerator:** referrals_completed  
**Denominator:** high_bp_identified  
**Formula:** referrals_completed / high_bp_identified

---

## Follow-up Completion Rate

**Definition:** Percent of participants with high BP who completed follow-up.

**Numerator:** followup_completed  
**Denominator:** high_bp_identified  
**Formula:** followup_completed / high_bp_identified
