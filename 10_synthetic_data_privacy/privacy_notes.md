# Privacy Notes for Healthcare Data

## Do Not Upload PHI

Do not upload data containing:

- patient names
- full dates of birth
- phone numbers
- email addresses
- medical record numbers
- addresses
- Social Security numbers
- account numbers
- exact service dates
- free-text notes containing identifiers

## Why Synthetic Data Helps

Synthetic data can mimic the structure of real healthcare data without exposing real patient information.

Useful for:

- tutorials
- dashboards
- testing ETL pipelines
- code demonstrations
- open-source examples

## Caution

Synthetic data may still need review if generated from real data.

For public GitHub repositories, use:
- manually created synthetic examples
- fully simulated datasets
- public-use datasets
- de-identified datasets with permission

## Best Practice

When in doubt, do not upload the dataset.
