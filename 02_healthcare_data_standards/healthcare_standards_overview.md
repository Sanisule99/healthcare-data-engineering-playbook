# Healthcare Data Standards Overview

## ICD-10

Used for diagnoses and billing classification.

## CPT

Used for procedures and services.

## LOINC

Used for laboratory tests and clinical observations.

## SNOMED CT

Used for clinical terminology and detailed clinical concepts.

## RxNorm

Used for medications.

## HL7

Messaging standard used for exchanging healthcare data.

## FHIR

Modern API-based interoperability standard.

## OMOP

Common data model often used for observational research.

## Why Standards Matter

Standards make healthcare data more interoperable, reusable, and analyzable.
